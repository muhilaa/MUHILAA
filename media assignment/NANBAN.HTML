The film stars Vijay in the titular role, alongside Srikanth, Jiiva, Sathyaraj, Ileana D'Cruz, Sathyan, Anuya, with S. J. Suryah and Vijay Vasanth in cameo appearances. It is a remake of the Rajkumar Hirani's 2009 Hindi-language film 3 Idiots, which itself is based on Chetan Bhagat's novel Five Point Someone. The film follows Venkat and Senthil, a wildlife photographer and an engineer respectively, who are on the search for their estranged classmate, Panchavan Parivendhan.
